Game Sprites
Assorted sprite animations I've made for games.
Game Sprites
I make a lot of animated sprites for games!

For the puzzle-platformer Inspectre, I rigged and animated the main character in various movement states. Furthermore, I gave several background assets animated states that added to the livelihood of the game.

For the rhythm game Snakes on a Plane, I designed several backgrounds and tiles for levels with different themes, each inspired by a different song.

For Walkie Talkie Space Ship, a project for CMS.611, I animated asteroids and a spaceship in the style of retro pixel art space invaders games.

3D Modeling
The 3D models, rigs, and animations I've made with Blender.
3D Modeling
I also enjoy making low-poly renders of various interesting objects in Blender.

Hot take: pastries are the best thing to have been invented since... forever. Sometimes I feel so overwhelmed by my appreciation for sugar, I can't help but model my current favorite sweet thing.

The clear-cut fold structure of origami has always made me think of it as being the real-life equivalent of low-poly modeling. So, sometimes I enjoy combining the two together and creating low-poly models of classic origami creatures.
