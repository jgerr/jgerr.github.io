fandomwalk is an interactive visualization that allows you to simulate the experience of being a fanfiction author, exploring the success you achieve--and how it compares to others' success--as a direct result of your narrative choices through data visualization.

when the cursor is blinking, type your choice from the list of possible options for each of several categories. press enter to register those choices, and continue to the next one.

click here to close
fandomwalk uses the anonymized fandom dataset compiled in 2017 by researchers at UWash, and was created by joanna gerr @ MIT (2020).

for the purposes of this project, the original dataset was needlessly large; i ran python scripts to cull the dataset down to something more managable, preserving only the data I felt would yield most interesting results, and limiting this viz to 5 large overarching categories, 8 fandoms per category, and 8000 works per each fandom--which 8000 were chosen via randomized sampling.
