All's Fair In Love and the Board
All's Fair In Love and the Board is a Visual Novel about CHESS, the Consultants for Hectic Emergencies and Solid Solutions. They're a legendary company; you're a recent graduate from the Prestigious Academy for Wholesome Normies. As a lowly PAWN, this journey to capture the hearts of your coworkers is an uphill battle.

I wrote, illustrated, coded, and composed this game. This game was made in the Ren'Py engine, and all composition was done using FL Studio.

Hell's Kitchen
Hell's Kitchen is a 24-page multisequential comic wherein you, a rookie chef, are making curry for the first time. ...or at least attempting to do so.

You can read it on my site digitally, or find me in the Real World™ and cop a tangible copy.
