Other Projects
In my spare time, I like making games, art, and tools. Here are some projects I've worked on in the past few years!
Plantdroid
A taskbar idle clicker game where you upgrade a robot to cultivate your infinitely procedural garden, developed solo in Unreal Engine 5.6.
All's Fair in Love and the Board
A visual novel about chess and capturing other piece('s hearts), featuring chess moves as choices, developed solo in Ren'Py.
Inspectre
A 2D puzzle-platformer (pw: uncle) made with Unity, in which a young lad must possess objects to traverse a trap-filled mansion.
MIT Mystery Hunt 2026
Designer and artist on website, Monarch creature compendium, and assorted creatures and puzzles for Cardinality's 2026 iteration of the MIT Mystery Hunt puzzle hunt.
CATS
A Python add-on for Blender that allows digital artists to easily create Non-Photorealistic Rendering styles with Photoshop-like layers UI.
Data Visualization
Two D3 visualizations I've made: PokeMonitor interactively displays spawn rates for the Kanto region, and Fandom Walk takes user input to replicate the fanfiction fandom experience
Hell's Kitchen
A 24-page choose-your-own-adventure comic, in which a rookie chef tries to make curry for the first time. Read it here.
