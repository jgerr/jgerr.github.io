Graphic Design

These are some of the designs I've made for student groups I am a part of at MIT. I have much experience and familiarity designing posters, and know how to convey important information in a readable and visually enganging manner. Furthermore, I really enjoy the design process! The middle "Next Big Shipper" image is my design for my student group that is building a space-themed maze on campus for the upcoming freshmen visitation weekend. It was a challenge to think of a subtle way to incorporate the maze into this space design, and when I eventually had the idea to make a circular galaxy-like maze, I was elated.

UI Design

These are some of the GUI mockups I made for my work with the CLEVR game project. Originally, we envisioned having 3 separate players outside the headset, and I made separate designs for each of these roles. However, we later decided to combine the basic functionalities of every outside role into one interface to make testing easier, so I iterated upon my old designs and refined them. The first 3 mockups depict the roles separated, and the last one shows their synthesis.
