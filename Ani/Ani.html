Mr. Balloon's Wild Ride

This most recent animation was done in TVPaint, a bitmap-based animation software. I combined the story of a lost-and-found balloon with piano accents to create a modern piece in the style of an older silent film.

Dream Sequence

This minute-long animation was made in Adobe Flash, and depicts the vividly colored scenarios one boy travels through as he dreams. The background recording of Chopin's Minute Waltz was performed by me.

Finders, Keepers

In this animation, two birds duke it out to claim rightful possession of a worm found lying upon the ground. Unfortunately, (spoiler alert!) the worm manages to escape from their collective clutches, and all that remains is the bitter taste of what could have been lunch. Made in Adobe Flash.

Gif Library

Over the last year, I've created a dozens of animated gifs depicting various actions and concepts for the Assistive Technology club at my school. These gifs have gone into e-books used by disabled children to help them link words and meanings together with greater ease. Anyone is free to use these gifs, so long as they give due credit to the creator: me!
