PLATES

PLATES is a nonprofit organization based in Northern Virginia. Their goal is to provide impovershed elementary students with nutritious food over extended breaks, such as vacation times in summer and winter, where school lunches aren't available for said students.

When designing the PLATES logo, I aimed to present their message in a visual way. Through incorporating the various food groups and superimposing them onto a plate, this logo showcases PLATES' health-conscious contribution to their cause.


Toshiba Exploravision

The Toshiba Exploravision Competition is a science competition for high school students. In the 2013-2014 cycle, the group I competed with recieved an honorable mention for our reasarch into using synthetic enzymes to assist Alzheimer's patients. This album is a compilation of a the site mockups I made for our run.
