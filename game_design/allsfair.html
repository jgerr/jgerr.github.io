♟️ All's Fair in Love and the Board
A visual novel about CHESS, unlike any you've ever seen. The Consultants for Hectic Emergencies and Solid Solutions (CHESS) have hired you, a recent graduate from the Prestigious Academy for Wholesome Normies (PAWN). But lo and behold, you've been tasked with capturing your coworker's hearts!
  • Scripted a custom chessboard dialogue-choice mechanic in Ren'Py
  • Featuring some meta-analysis on the futility of living life bounded by arbitrary rules, as a PAWN does on a chessboard
  • Coded in Ren'Py, music composition in FL Studio, and art created in Clip Studio Paint
