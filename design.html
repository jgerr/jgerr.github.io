The aMAZEing Space
Last year, I designed the aesthetics for a student-run maze during campus preview week.

Our theme was 'space', so I placed a rocket in the center of the logo and surrounded it with a circular maze resembling planetary rings.

The maze itself mirrored this structure: a rocket-like tower in the center, and celestial bodies painted around the exterior.
